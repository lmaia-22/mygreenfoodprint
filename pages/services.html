What we offer!


MyGreenFoodPrint is an app developed to help you to change your carbon footprint through food. At the same time you are making the world a better place and changing habits to stop climate changes, you will be also implementing healthier food habits which will impact your life quality.

MyGreenFoodPrint has the following services (functionalities):

Food footprint calculator

So you know your impact!

Food macronutrients and calories calculator

This way you do not have to decrease the amount of proteins you consume!

Hints/Suggestions of daily menus with low carbon footprint

It will be so easy to eat better!

Track of your progress

See your changes in terms of GHGs and share it with your friends!

Partnerships with local producers

So you can have your fresh products directly from your area, with low carbon footprint.

Donations

Through MyGreenFoodPrint you can contribute monetarily to projects that have a positive

Impact in our world and work to reduce the GHG from our planet

This is a way for you to net your carbon footprint!
